1. Petroleum and petroleum products: Saudi Arabia is the world's largest exporter of oil and it relies heavily on crude oil and oil products for its export revenue.
2. Natural gas: The country has substantial reserves of natural gas and exports a significant amount of it, primarily as liquefied natural gas (LNG).
3. Chemicals: Saudi Arabia has a sizable chemical industry, producing a variety of chemicals including fertilizers, plastics, and synthetic fibers, which are exported worldwide.
4. Phosphate Rock: The Kingdom is one of the world's leading producers of phosphate rock, used in the production of fertilizers and detergents.
5. Base Metals: Saudi Arabia exports base metals such as aluminum, zinc, copper, and lead.
6. Gold and Precious Metals: The country has significant gold reserves and is a major exporter of gold and precious metals.
7. Agricultural products: Despite being a desert country, Saudi Arabia exports some agricultural products, including dates, vegetables, and fruits.
8. Livestock and their products: The livestock sector in Saudi Arabia is well-developed and the country exports live animals as well as dairy products such as milk powder and cheese.
