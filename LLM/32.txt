To apply for an Indian Passport online, follow these steps:

1. Visit the official website of the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) or Passport Seva portal at www.passportindia.gov.in

2. Click on "Apply for Fresh Passport/Re-issue of Passport" under the 'Online Application/Form Submission' section.

3. Fill out the application form, providing all required details such as personal information, address, travel details, and so forth. Ensure all information is accurate to avoid any delays or complications in the process.

4. Upload necessary documents like photographs (passport size), address proof, ID proof, etc., according to the specific guidelines provided on the website.

5. Pay the prescribed fee using online payment methods such as credit/debit card, net banking, or other available options.

6. Schedule an appointment at a Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or Regional Passport Office (RPO) for biometric data collection and verification of original documents.

7. Visit the appointed PSK or RPO on the given date, along with all original supporting documents, for verification and biometric data capture.

8. Check the status of your application regularly using the passport tracking service available on the website.

9. Once the passport is ready, you will receive an SMS/email notification to collect it from the designated PSK or RPO. Bring the acknowledgement receipt and identity proof for collection.
