The Magenta Line, also known as the Janakpuri West-Mandawali corridor, is a part of the Delhi Metro Rail Network. It connects Dwarka Sector 21 station with RIT Zone (Mandawali) in the north-eastern parts of Delhi. This line plays a significant role in reducing traffic congestion and providing an efficient mode of transportation to thousands of commuters daily, improving their travel experience significantly. It also serves important landmarks such as the Dwarka Sector 21 Metro Station, the Mahavir Enclave, and the Indira Gandhi International Airport (IGIA) among others.
