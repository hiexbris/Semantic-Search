The common side effects of metformin may include:

1. Stomach upset (nausea, vomiting, diarrhea)
2. Metallic taste in the mouth
3. Loss of appetite
4. Weight loss
5. Headache
6. Low blood sugar symptoms, if taken with certain other diabetes medications (fast heartbeat, sweating, confusion, irritability, hunger, dizziness, or tingling hands/feet)
7. Lactic acidosis is a rare but serious side effect of metformin which may include symptoms such as: weakness, unusual muscle pain, trouble breathing, cold hands and feet, feeling dizzy or lightheaded, rapid heart rate, or unusual tiredness. If you experience any of these symptoms, seek immediate medical attention.
