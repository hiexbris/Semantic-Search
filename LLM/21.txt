The typical recommended dose of Paracetamol (Acetaminophen) for adults is:
- Oral Dosage Forms (Tablets/Capsules): 325 mg to 650 mg every 4 to 6 hours, not exceeding 3,900 mg in 24 hours.
- Rectal Suppository: 125 mg to 325 mg every 4 to 6 hours, not exceeding 750 mg in 24 hours.
- Liquid Suspension/Oral Solution: 10 mg/kg to 15 mg/kg every 4 to 6 hours, not exceeding 75 mg/kg in 24 hours. It's important to note that these dosages may vary depending on the individual's weight and medical condition, so always consult a healthcare professional before taking any medication.
