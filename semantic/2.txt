1. Moringa leaves are highly nutritious and contain protein, essential amino acids, 27 vitamins, and 46 antioxidants. They have anti-inflammatory properties and can improve skin texture, reduce fine lines, and aid in hair growth.

2. Moringa leaves are rich in antioxidants like quercetin, chlorogenic acid, vitamin C, and beta-carotene that may help protect against chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and cancer.

3. Moringa leaves can help stabilize blood sugar levels due to the presence of isothiocyanates. Consuming moringa every day may lead to modest reductions in blood sugar and cholesterol levels.

4. Moringa leaves are beneficial for eye health as they contain high concentrations of vitamin A.

5. It's important to consult a healthcare professional before taking Moringa oleifera, especially if you are pregnant or taking medications to treat diabetes, high blood pressure, and thyroid disorders.

6. Limited human studies have examined the long-term effects of Moringa, but no adverse side effects in humans have been detected yet. It's recommended not to take more than 70 mg per day to prevent a buildup of toxins.

7. More research is needed to understand the possible benefits and long-term effects of Moringa on weight loss, autoimmune conditions, and other health aspects.
