1. In India, Indira Gandhi International Airport (Delhi) is the busiest airport in terms of passenger traffic, handling 73,673,708 passengers in 2024.

2. In Mumbai, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport is the primary airport, but it handles fewer passengers than Delhi's Indira Gandhi International Airport.

3. The largest airport in Asia by area is Rajiv Gandhi International Airport in Hyderabad, Telangana, covering 5,500 acres, although Noida International Airport in Jewar, India, will become the largest upon completion.
