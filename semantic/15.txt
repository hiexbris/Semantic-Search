The lyrics to Jana Gana Mana are as follows:

Jaya he, Jaya he, Jaya he, Jaya jaya jaya jaya he.
Jana-gana-mana adhināyaka jaya hē Bhārat-bhāgya-vidhātā.
Jaya hē, Jaya hē, Jaya hē, Jaya jaya jaya jaya hē.

Translation:

1. Victory, victory, victory, victory to thee!
2. Thou art the ruler of the minds of all the people,
   Dispenser of India's destiny.
3. Victory, victory, victory, victory to thee!
4. Thou art the ruler of the minds of all the people,
   Dispenser of India's destiny.
