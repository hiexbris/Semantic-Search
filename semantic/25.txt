The significance of the Magenta Line Metro in Delhi is that it is a key part of the Delhi Metro rapid transit system, being the first driverless metro line in India. It serves Terminal 1 of the Indira Gandhi International Airport directly and spans 40.26 kilometers (25.02 mi) across South Delhi, with interchanges available at various points with other lines within the Delhi Metro network. Notable features include India's tallest escalator at Janakpuri West station and the only restricted civilian movement station by the military at Shankar Vihar metro station.
